BandwagonHost VPS Guide

Posted on 2020-10-21 | In Technology , Computer Science |
Words count in article: 101

Preface

A virtual private server (VPS) is a virtual machine sold as a service by an Internet hosting service. The virtual dedicated server (VDS) also has a similar meaning(More Information refers to Wikipedia).

Cancellation & Refund

STEP1: Login in BandwagonHost and move into Client Area.

STEP2: Find “refund” in the “billing” navigation, then submit cancellation&refund request.

STEP3: After clicking the button, BandwagonHost would automatically send an email to you to confirm your request.

STEP4: Waiting for 15-45 minutes, you would recieve an email containing the result.
