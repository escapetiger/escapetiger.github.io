A virtual private server (VPS) is a virtual machine sold as a service by an Internet hosting service. The virtual dedicated server (VDS) also has a similar meaning(More Information refers to Wikipedia).

Cancellation & Refund#

STEP1: Login in BandwagonHost and move into Client Area.

STEP2: Find “refund” in the “billing” navigation, then submit cancellation&refund request. {% asset_img fig1.png Fig1%}

STEP3: After clicking the button, BandwagonHost would automatically send an email to you to confirm your request. {% asset_img fig2.png Fig2: Your reply must ONLY contain “I agree”%}

STEP4: Waiting for 15-45 minutes, you would recieve an email containing the result. {% asset_img fig3.png Fig3: Succeed!%}
